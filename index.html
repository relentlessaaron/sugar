DETERMINATION

Determination is but your intention coupled with your drive, ambition and want to succeed at something. Our young champions are INTENSE! And they make the Sugar Bert Brand very special.





Age: Nothin But A Number

The SugarBert boxing community is busy with active boxing families from all around the world. They are diverse, they are full of desire, and they color our superstages with life. We appreciate all of our boxers and ethnicities!





The Kids Earn Their "Courage Stripes" Here

They are so determined and you can't help but to love their passion to win against all odds.




For Some,  Boxing Is Breathing

When soemone devotes their life to something, reaching above & beyond those with ALL their faculties and capabilities, one can't help but to be inspired and attracted to the  energy that fuels such passiona and determination. SugarBert Boxing  is proud to  have such champions in our community.




The Big Stage

We're not your average boxing evernt: 3 or more rings of action during 3 to 5 days. It all happens simultaneously under the same roof, in the same arena. There's a non-stop electricity in the air that keeps your eyeballs busy!








Passion

To those who eat, sleep and dream boxing, we raise our glasses and tip our hats to you. Its you that we are here to encourage, to promote and to serve. Thank you from all the staff at SugeBert Boxing.








Family Support System

When families and communities come great distances to cheer-on their champions it creates a dynamic that is unmatched. It is both spontaneous and inspiring. Thank you to all the families in the Sugar Bert Boxing community. We are ALL family.







Professionals At Work

Our professional trainers, referees, coaches and doctors are in attendance to see that all boxers are protected and evenly matched and judged. We treasure our officials and event staff.





3 Rounds of Electricity

The Corner
