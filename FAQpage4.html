Sugar Bert Boxing Promotions FAQ's

Your frequently asked questions are answered right here.

  • QUESTION: If we boxed at a qualifier at one weight class are we held to that weight class at nationals? ANSWER: No, your weight for competition at Nationals will be declared upon surrender of your passbook during registration.
  • QUESTION: If I cannot come to the tournament, will there be someone that can work my athletes corner? ANSWER: Yes, please let a staff member know at registration of your needs.

  • QUESTION: Is head gear with cheek protectors allowed or will open face only be accepted?
    ANSWER: Open face headgear is required for Junior, Youth, & Elite divisions. Masters must wear AIBA/USAB approved Masters headgear. All other divisions may wear cheek protectors.
  • QUESTION: Will master bouts have the option of time for their matches?
    ANSWER: All Masters bouts, tournament or matched, will consist of 3 rounds 1.5 minutes in duration.
  • QUESTION: Does a boxer weigh in each day?
    ANSWER: Athletes will only weigh-in and have a pre-bout medical exam on the days they compete.

  • QUESTION: Is there a weight allowance?
    ANSWER: No, zero-tolerance will be given for an athlete being overweight for the weight category in which they are entered into the tournament.

  • QUESTION: Does my boxer need to be present at the start of weigh-ins?
    ANSWER: Yes, if you are not present at the time your name is called, you will be disqualified.​

  • QUESTION: Can I use gym wraps when we compete? ANSWER:  Valpeau or gauze & tape are the only permissible wraps for competition.

  • QUESTION: Does the coach need credentials to work the corner?
    ANSWER: All coaches will receive credentials at registration which must be worn in the FOP at all times.

  • QUESTION: Will my athlete compete more than once each day?
    ANSWER: No, all athletes will compete no more than once in a 24 hour period.

  • QUESTION: Can I wear whatever uniform I choose in the ring?
    ANSWER: Athletes are required to wear loose fitting trunks with a contrasting waist band and a sleeveless boxing shirt. The trunks may not have flaps, sequins, fringes, or be gladiator/thai in style. No part of the athlete’s uniform may have nicknames, phrases, slogans, or tape of any kind.

  • QUESTION: Can I wear a mouthpiece with any color or design?
    ANSWER: Mouthpieces may not be orange, pink, or red in any shade.

  • QUESTION: I brought my own boxing equipment, can I use it?
    ANSWER: Competition gloves will be provided. All other equipment will be the responsibility of the athlete.
